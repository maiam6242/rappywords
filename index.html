  1. TO PLAY:
  2. Pick captains, they'll pick players.
  3. Captains choose to get easy, medium, or hard words to rhyme.
  4. In order of 1st picked to last, each player
    • makes a sentence ending with a rhyme of the word.
    • or says "skip", then passes the mic til time's up.
    • rhyme for easy words get 1 point, medium words get 2, hard words get 3.
    • points are added when a player presses the button to count them or pass.
    • a team gets an extra rhyme score added at the end of the round if more than 2 players do rhymes back to back, and no one passes.
  5. Or a captain can choose to do trivia questions, where each player:
    • submits an answer, then the next player does until time's up.
    • correct answers give a team 2 points.
    • incorrect answers take away a point.
    • a team gets a bonus streak for a round when it has no incorrect answers.
  6. The game ends after 3 rounds.
  7. The team with the most points wins.
  8. Players can customize elements of the game and/or adjust score discrepencies uing the prompts on the screen.
