This is the
origin story

In March 2021, The STEMcees began working with THHT.

We played an ice breaker game that was inspired by the board game Rhyme Antics. Everyone did not quite remember the rules from playing the Rhyme Antics game. On the fly, some of the THHT crew thought up a rhyming game we could play over Zoom and that first play led to this game. We talking about what a Rappy Words app would look like, what words it would use, and how we could prototype it. Here we are.
